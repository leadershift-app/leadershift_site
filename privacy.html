Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 15, 2026 · Effective: February 15, 2026

Introduction

LeaderShift provides confidential AI-powered peer support and professional development for senior public safety leaders. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, your rights and choices, and how to contact us.

By using LeaderShift, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

Phone Number

What: Your mobile phone number

Why: Used for authentication via SMS one-time passcode

Who Processes: Supabase (our authentication provider)

Retention: Until you delete your account

Rank

What: Your professional rank (Lieutenant, Captain, Chief, Sheriff, Superintendent)

Why: Provides rank-appropriate professional development guidance

Storage: Stored locally on your device

Retention: Until you change it or delete the app

Conversation Content

What: Your messages and the AI-generated responses

Why: To provide peer support and professional development services

Storage: Encrypted on your device only

Retention: Until you delete individual conversations or the app

B. Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: iOS version, app version, device model
  • Network Information: IP address, approximate location (city/state from IP), ISP
  • Usage Data: Conversation timestamps, messages per session, feature usage
  • Technical Data: Error logs (crash reports), performance metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

Provide Services: Authenticate via SMS, send messages to Anthropic's Claude API for AI-powered peer support, adapt guidance to your rank, store conversations encrypted on your device.

Improve Services: Analyze usage patterns (aggregated, anonymous), fix bugs, develop new features.

Security & Compliance: Detect and prevent abuse, respond to legal requests, ensure reliability.

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your data
  • Use your conversations for advertising
  • Share your information with your employer or agency
  • Train AI models on your conversations (without explicit opt-in)

2b. In-App Feedback Collection

LeaderShift may prompt you to provide feedback about your experience.

What We Collect: Star ratings, thumbs up/down on responses, optional text feedback (200 characters max), usage milestones, professional impact assessments.

How It Works: All feedback is stored encrypted on your device until you choose to send it. You control when feedback is shared. Each prompt includes explicit opt-in. You can dismiss or opt out in Settings.

How We Use It: Improve support quality, identify bugs, prioritize features, measure impact.

Feedback is not shared with third parties, not linked to your conversations, and only aggregated anonymous statistics are used publicly.

3. How We Share Your Information

A. Service Providers

Supabase

What: Phone number, authentication tokens

Why: SMS authentication

Location: United States

Cloudflare

What: IP address, conversation content (in transit), device info

Why: Hosts our API that routes messages to Anthropic

Retention: Real-time tail (under 1 hour); persistent logs disabled

Anthropic

What: Your messages (prompts) and AI-generated responses

Why: Provides AI-powered peer support via Claude API

Retention: Per Anthropic's data retention policy

B. Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law: court orders, subpoenas, law enforcement requests with valid legal process, or to prevent imminent harm.

If we receive a legal request, we will notify you (unless legally prohibited), challenge overly broad requests, and provide only the minimum information required.

C. Business Transfers

If LeaderShift is acquired or merged, your information may be transferred. You will be notified via email or app notification.

We Do NOT Share With:

  • Your employer or police agency
  • Advertisers or marketing companies
  • Data brokers
  • Social media platforms
  • Any third party for their own purposes

4. Data Storage & Security

On Your Device

  • All conversations encrypted with AES-256-GCM
  • Encryption key stored in iOS Secure Enclave (Keychain)
  • Database excluded from iCloud backup
  • Delete individual conversations anytime or delete the entire app to erase all local data

In Transit

  • TLS 1.3 encryption for all network requests
  • Certificate pinning (prevents man-in-the-middle attacks)

On Our Servers

  • We do not store conversation content on our servers
  • IP address in Cloudflare logs: real-time only, not persisted
  • Error logs: auto-deleted after 7 days (metadata only)

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access: Request what data we have about you (minimal)
  • Delete Conversations: Tap and hold any conversation, then Delete
  • Delete Account: Settings, then Delete Account (removes phone number from Supabase and local data)
  • Account deletion does not remove data from Anthropic (contact them directly)

7. Children's Privacy

LeaderShift is not for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover we have, we will delete it immediately. Contact privacy@leadershift.app if you believe a child provided information to us.

8. State-Specific Privacy Rights

California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

  • Right to Know: What personal information we collect
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion (with limitations above)
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We don't sell your data
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We won't treat you differently for exercising rights

Exercise rights via email: privacy@leadershift.app or in-app: Settings, then Privacy Request.

European Union Residents (GDPR)

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: "Right to be forgotten"
  • Portability: Receive data in machine-readable format
  • Object: Object to certain processing
  • Restrict: Limit how we use your data

Data transfers to the United States are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs). Exercise rights at privacy@leadershift.app (response time: 30 days).

Other States

Nevada, Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah residents have similar rights. Contact privacy@leadershift.app.

9. International Users

LeaderShift is based in the United States. If you use LeaderShift from outside the US, your data will be transferred to and processed in the US. US privacy laws may differ from your country. By using LeaderShift, you consent to this transfer.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in practices, legal requirements, or new features. Material changes will receive 30 days advance notice via email or in-app notification.

11. Security Safeguards

  • AES-256-GCM encryption
  • TLS 1.3 for network requests
  • iOS Keychain for key storage
  • Database excluded from iCloud backup
  • Limited employee access, regular security audits, incident response plan

No system is 100% secure. While we use industry-standard protections, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. What This App Is NOT

  • Not a substitute for professional counseling
  • Not emergency services (call 911 for emergencies)
  • Not legal advice
  • Not subject to HIPAA

LeaderShift IS: Peer support and professional development, confidential (not shared with employer), AI-powered (not human therapist).
