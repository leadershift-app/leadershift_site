Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know, from privacy to pricing.

Can my department see my conversations?
No. This is the foundation of everything we built. Your conversations are never logged and you are never identified. We have no backdoors for administrators or agencies. There is no record for your department to access and nothing that can be subpoenaed through departmental channels. You can delete your data at any time and it is wiped completely. This is not a policy. It is how the system is architected.
What is LeaderShift?
LeaderShift is an AI-powered coaching and professional development tool built specifically for public safety professionals. It helps you navigate career progression, leadership challenges, and the daily realities of the job with guidance that understands rank structures, department culture, and the unique pressures you face.
Is this therapy?
No. LeaderShift is a coaching and professional development tool, not licensed therapy. It focuses on career development, leadership skills, and navigating workplace challenges. If you are dealing with trauma, PTSD, or clinical issues, we encourage you to work with a licensed professional. Many people find professional development and clinical care complement each other well.
How is this different from ChatGPT?
We built LeaderShift because generic AI fails public safety professionals. Our testing showed tools like ChatGPT routinely give advice that violates policy, ignores chain of command, or misses critical safety considerations. LeaderShift understands what a Sergeant is, how a Captain communicates differently with a Chief than with a Rookie, and the specific realities of your work environment. We speak to officers, not about them.
Will using this affect my fitness-for-duty status?
No. LeaderShift is a professional development tool. Using it is no different from reading a leadership book or attending a conference. It does not create clinical records, and since your department has no access to your conversations, your use cannot be factored into fitness-for-duty evaluations or affect your career standing.
I'm a patrol officer, not a supervisor. Is this for me?
Yes. Leadership development does not start at promotion. Some of the most consequential career decisions happen at the patrol level: whether to go for Sergeant, how to handle a difficult FTO relationship, how to build the skills that promotion boards actually look for, and how to stay motivated when the job gets heavy. LeaderShift meets you where you are.
How does LeaderShift benefit the communities officers serve?
When public safety professionals invest in their own leadership development, the effects ripple outward. Better communication skills, more thoughtful decision-making, stronger self-awareness, and greater resilience under pressure all translate directly into how officers interact with the people they serve. Supporting the people who carry the badge is one of the most direct ways to strengthen public safety for everyone.
How much does it cost?
$9.99 per month through the Apple App Store. Cancel anytime. Your subscription gives you unlimited access to all coaching and professional development features.
Is my data used to train AI models?
No. We do not train our AI on your personal conversations. Your data stays your data. Personalization happens within your individual experience, not by feeding your information into a broader training dataset.
Who built this?
LeaderShift was founded by Eric Lohela and Kevin Magnan. Eric brings twenty years in government consulting with a dedicated focus towards public safety since 2019, having worked with dozens of agencies from Michigan State Police to LAPD. Kevin is a former sworn officer with a Master's in data and analytics who ran a statewide crime lab before designing the AI technology behind LeaderShift. Our advisory team includes a retired deputy chief with twenty-three years of service who held every role inside a law enforcement organization and a strategic partner from the NYU Policing Project. We are an independent company. That independence means our only obligation is to you.
Can my department purchase LeaderShift for our personnel?
We are exploring institutional partnerships for agencies that want to support their people's professional development. If your department is interested, contact us at support@leadershift.app. Individual subscriptions remain completely private regardless of how they are funded.
What devices does LeaderShift work on?
LeaderShift is currently available for iPhone and iPad (iOS 15.0+). Android is coming soon. We recommend using your personal device rather than department-issued equipment to ensure your conversations remain entirely under your control.

The LeaderShift Constitution

Three Principles. No Compromises.

Every AI system has values, whether its builders name them or not. We chose to name ours. The LeaderShift Constitution governs every interaction, every design decision, and every boundary our platform maintains. Three co-equal pillars. No hierarchy between them. Each one constraining and enabling the others.

01
Private

Real professional growth requires honest self-reflection. And honest self-reflection requires knowing that what you say stays between you and the tool you are working with. In a profession defined by constant documentation and institutional scrutiny, privacy is not a feature. It is the foundation that makes everything else possible.

How This Shows Up

No one sees your conversations. Your interactions with LeaderShift are never logged and you are never identified. There is no record for anyone to access — not your department, not us, not anyone.
No departmental access. There are no backdoors, no admin portals, no data exports. Nothing can be subpoenaed through departmental channels because there is nothing to produce. This matters in an era where digital interactions increasingly live in discoverable systems.
Your data, your control. Delete it anytime and it is wiped completely. No approval process. No retention policies working against you. Using LeaderShift is no different from reading a leadership book on your own time.
Independent by design. We are not a department tool. We are a personal professional development platform. That distinction is structural and legal, not semantic. It is what allows us to serve your interests first.
◆
02
Honest

You do not need another tool that tells you what you want to hear. You need a partner that tells you what you need to hear, with the care and precision that helps you navigate a complex world. Honesty without empathy is cruelty. Empathy without honesty is cowardice. We practice both together.

How This Shows Up

Direct feedback. When your approach has blind spots, LeaderShift names them respectfully. A good partner does not let you walk into a promotion board underprepared because telling you felt uncomfortable.
Honest about limitations. LeaderShift will tell you what it does not know, what it cannot do, and when you need a human professional instead. We will never pretend to be therapy, legal counsel, or a substitute for clinical care.
No performance theater. We do not inflate your readiness, minimize real challenges, or offer false reassurance. The job is too serious for that.
Candor is kind. Withholding honest feedback is the unkind act. Every interaction is built on this principle.
◈
03
Useful

Useful means industry-specific and built with guidance and feedback from patrol officers through executive leadership to manage the real-world scenarios you face on the job and when you take the day home.

How This Shows Up

Context-aware guidance. LeaderShift knows that "time blocking" means something different when you work 12-hour rotating shifts with mandatory overtime and court appearances. Generic productivity advice does not survive first contact with the job.
Rank-calibrated. The coaching a patrol officer considering promotion needs is different from what a Captain preparing for a Chief's role needs. LeaderShift scales its depth, language, and framing to match where you are in your career.
Actionable, not theoretical. Every interaction aims to leave you with something you can do, say, prepare for, or think differently about. Practical next steps, not abstract wisdom.
Evolves with you. LeaderShift remembers your goals, your challenges, and where you left off. It uses a stable, clear approach that builds rapport over time, becoming more personalized the more you use it. And behind it, a team constantly working to make every conversation better.

Our Story

Why LeaderShift Exists

Public safety professionals carry a weight that most people will never fully understand. The decisions they make under pressure, the careers they build inside complex organizations, the line they walk between duty and personal identity. LeaderShift exists because the career support available to these professionals has not kept pace with the demands placed on them in this country.

01
The Challenge

Modern policing demands leaders it does not fully equip.

The profession asks officers to be crisis negotiators, community liaisons, legal scholars, team managers, media-facing representatives, and tactical operators, often within the same shift. Officers do receive training as they advance — academy instruction, leadership off-sites, specialized courses, and additional support at each career stage. But there is a gap between what formal programs deliver and what officers actually need to navigate the complex organizational realities of their careers.

That gap widens at every step up the chain of command. The higher you go, the fewer people are available to talk to honestly about what the role actually demands.

The people who need the most strategic support and insight have the fewest confidential places to pursue it. That is the paradox of leadership in public safety.

The profession faces a real retention challenge. Experienced officers leave, taking institutional knowledge that cannot be replaced. Providing officers with tools to support their career progression — outside the direct demands of constitutional policing and service delivery — is one of the most strategic investments we can make in the profession's ability to serve communities for the long term.

02
The Gap

Generic AI fails public safety professionals.

When we tested consumer AI tools with real public safety scenarios, the results were not just unhelpful. They were dangerous. Tools like ChatGPT routinely provided advice that violated policy, ignored chain of command, or missed critical safety considerations. In our safety testing, generic AI even offered guidance on how to doctor evidence and avoid constitutional policing practices.

The challenges of unregulated AI and off-the-shelf solutions go beyond bad advice. Officers need timely guidance on how to have complex conversations. They need space to share how they are feeling and vent after a hard shift. They need the ability to reorient themselves when they need to switch modes between professional demands and the rest of their lives. Generic tools were not designed for any of this.

There is nothing like this coming from agencies to support officers on their own terms, and generic tools do not work. LeaderShift exists to provide this strategic tool on your personal device, for conversations about how to show up at your best.

The coaching industry has seen explosive growth, with platforms serving executives and managers across corporate environments. But none of them speak to the specific realities of public safety: the rank structures, the operational tempo, the legal environment, the culture. It is a different world, and it demands a purpose-built solution.

03
The Shift

The name is the mission.

We named the company LeaderShift because the word captures everything we are building.

The work shift. The hours on duty, the mental transition on the drive home, and the moments between calls when you process what just happened and prepare for what comes next.

The career shift. Every promotion is a shift in identity, not just a change in title — from executing to leading, from following to shaping the culture of an entire organization.

The cultural shift. Public safety operates in an increasingly complex environment with growing scrutiny. The profession is evolving, and the leaders navigating that change need tools that help officers and communities bridge the gap around trust.

LeaderShift is a companion across all of these — from the work shift through career coaching conversations to the complexity of leading in a profession that is changing in real time.
04
The Approach

Trust is the moat. Everything else follows.

We chose to build for the individual professional first, because trust in this space cannot be mandated from the top down. It has to be earned one conversation at a time.

The privacy architecture exists because professionals need to know their reflections stay private. The independence from departmental affiliation exists because the moment we become "the department's tool," we lose the ability to serve the individual's actual interests. In an era where digital interactions increasingly live in discoverable systems, officers need to know that their professional development conversations are theirs alone.

05
The Vision

Stronger leaders. Safer communities.

Every officer who navigates a difficult organizational relationship more effectively makes their team stronger. Every leader who prepares thoroughly for a promotion board raises the quality of command. Every professional who develops greater resilience stays in the profession longer, carrying institutional knowledge that cannot be replaced.

Leaders who invest in their own growth communicate more effectively and make better decisions, which directly supports the communities they serve. Supporting the people who carry the badge makes everyone safer.

LeaderShift is focused on public safety and corrections today, with a vision that extends to all first responders.

That is the shift we are building toward.
